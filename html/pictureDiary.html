PictureDiary

PictureDiary, an android application that allows users to take photographs which will save their Geo location, plus gives them the option to add content to the image. Once an entry has been saved they can view the location of that image on a map. This version of the application is not yet released to Google Play; however it can be downloaded with the link bellow (PhoneGap server link). The application will be published to Google Play once it has password protection and image encryption to give users some privacy with their PictureDiary.


The application will soon be on the Google play store, however that will be done when the application has image protection. At the moment the download is located on the phone gap servers.
